🚺 SafeRoute — Smart Women Safety Navigation Platform

Empowering safer travel through intelligent route guidance, unsafe-area detection, and emergency support.

🌍 Overview

SafeRoute is a modern women-safety focused navigation web application designed to help users travel more safely and confidently.

Unlike traditional navigation systems that only optimize for distance and travel time, SafeRoute prioritizes personal safety by highlighting potentially unsafe areas, providing safety-aware route insights, and enabling quick emergency support features.

The platform combines:

🗺️ Interactive maps
⚠️ Unsafe area visualization
📍 Community-driven safety reports
🛡️ Route safety scoring
🆘 SOS emergency sharing

to create a smarter and safer navigation experience.

✨ Key Features
🗺️ Smart Route Navigation

Get interactive route guidance with live map visualization.

⚠️ Unsafe Area Highlighting

Unsafe or poorly lit areas are visually marked on the map to increase awareness.

🛡️ Route Safety Score

Analyzes route conditions and generates a safety score for safer decision-making.

📍 Community Safety Reports

Users can report unsafe zones, isolated streets, harassment-prone areas, and poor lighting conditions.

🆘 Emergency SOS Support

Quickly share your live location with trusted contacts during emergencies.

💡 Smart Safety Tips

Context-aware safety recommendations based on travel conditions and time.

🎨 Modern UI/UX

Built with a responsive glassmorphism-inspired interface for a clean and modern experience.
